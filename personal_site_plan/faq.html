What type of fabric is used for hand embroidery?

Cotton canvas is a good option to start with. It is a fine fabric, with a regular weave and very economical, easy to find in any fabric store. The fabrics used for embroidery are: the so-called brown canvas, glasilla or cotonet. They are unbleached natural cotton fabrics, with a closed weave and not very thick.
